## Research Summary

Bakuchiol is the hottest emerging active ingredient in skincare - a natural, plant-derived retinol alternative extracted from Psoralea Corylifolia (Babchi) seeds. It provides retinol-like anti-aging benefits without photosensitivity, irritation, or pregnancy concerns. Market demand has exploded with 300%+ search growth, making this a critical category for comprehensive ingredient coverage.

**Key Benefits:**
- Retinol-like anti-aging effects without irritation
- Safe for daytime use (no photosensitivity)
- Pregnancy-safe and vegan
- Clinically proven efficacy
- Natural and plant-derived
- Suitable for sensitive skin
