# Research: Collagen & Collagen Boosters Suppliers

**Research Date:** December 11, 2025  
**Category:** Raw Materials > Collagen & Collagen Boosters  
**Target:** 10-15 suppliers  
**Status:** In Progress

## Overview

Collagen and collagen-boosting ingredients are essential actives in anti-aging and skin health formulations. This research covers three main categories:

1. **Marine Collagen** - Derived from fish skin/scales, Type I collagen
2. **Vegan Collagen Alternatives** - Biodesigned, plant-based, or synthetic alternatives
3. **Collagen Peptides** - Hydrolyzed collagen for enhanced bioavailability

## Market Context

- **Anti-Aging Focus:** Collagen production decreases with age, driving demand for collagen-boosting ingredients
- **Sustainability Trend:** Growing demand for vegan and biodesigned collagen alternatives
- **Bioavailability:** Hydrolyzed collagen peptides offer superior absorption
- **Applications:** Serums, creams, masks, supplements, color cosmetics
