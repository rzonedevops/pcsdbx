## Overview
Adaptogens are non-toxic plant substances that help the body adapt to stress and promote homeostasis. In skincare, adaptogens like ashwagandha, reishi mushroom, ginseng, holy basil, and rhodiola are gaining popularity for their stress-relieving, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties.
