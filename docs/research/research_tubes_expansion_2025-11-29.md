## Market Overview

Tube packaging is one of the most versatile and popular formats in the cosmetics industry. Tubes are used for:
- Facial creams and moisturizers
- Serums and treatments
- Hair care products
- Body care products
- Sunscreen and protective products
- Toothpaste and oral care

Tubes offer excellent product protection, precise dispensing, and strong branding opportunities. The market includes plastic tubes, laminate tubes, and aluminum tubes in various sizes and configurations.

## Tube Types & Specifications

### 1. Plastic Tubes

**Material:** Polypropylene (PP), Polyethylene (PE), Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET)

**Advantages:**
- Lightweight and cost-effective
- Wide range of colors and finishes
- Excellent barrier properties
- Customizable designs

**Common Sizes:** 15ml, 30ml, 50ml, 100ml, 150ml

**Applications:** Creams, gels, serums, body care

### 2. Laminate Tubes

**Material:** Aluminum foil + plastic layers

**Advantages:**
- Superior barrier protection
- Extended shelf life
- Premium appearance
- Excellent for sensitive products

**Common Sizes:** 30ml, 50ml, 75ml, 100ml, 150ml

**Applications:** Premium creams, serums, treatments

### 3. Aluminum Tubes

**Material:** Pure aluminum

**Advantages:**
- Maximum barrier protection
- Premium appearance
- Recyclable
- Excellent for luxury products

**Common Sizes:** 15ml, 30ml, 50ml, 75ml, 100ml

**Applications:** Luxury creams, serums, premium treatments
