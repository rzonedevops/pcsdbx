## 2. Senolytic Actives (Cellular Senescence Targeting)

### Market Overview
Senolytics target and eliminate senescent cells ("zombie cells") that stop dividing but remain metabolically active, releasing inflammatory signals (SASP - Senescence-Associated Secretory Phenotype) that accelerate aging. This represents a fundamental shift from surface-level anti-aging to cellular-level rejuvenation.

### Key Ingredients & Suppliers

#### **Quercetin (Multiple Suppliers)**
- **Description:** Flavonoid found in fruits, vegetables, and plants
- **Mechanism:** Targets and eliminates senescent cells
- **Applications:** Anti-aging serums and creams
- **Potential Suppliers:** 
  - Natural extract suppliers (botanical extract category)
  - Specialty active ingredient manufacturers
- **Category:** Senolytic Actives

#### **Fisetin (Multiple Suppliers)**
- **Description:** Flavonoid with senolytic activity
- **Evidence:** Shown to reduce signs of aging in animal studies
- **Applications:** Anti-aging formulations for skin health improvement
- **Potential Suppliers:**
  - Specialty active ingredient manufacturers
  - Natural extract suppliers
- **Category:** Senolytic Actives

#### **Natural Senolytic Extracts**
The following natural extracts show senolytic potential:

1. **Grape Extract** - Antioxidant and potential senolytic properties
2. **Alpine Willowherb** - Anti-inflammatory and senolytic activity
3. **Green Tea Extract (EGCG)** - Polyphenols with cellular renewal properties
4. **Pomegranate Extract** - Antioxidant and anti-aging benefits
5. **Isatis tinctoria (Woad Extract)** - Research shows senolytic effect on senescent human skin fibroblasts (NIH study)

## 3. Autophagy Activators

### Market Overview
Autophagy is the cellular "self-cleaning" process where cells break down and recycle damaged components. Activating autophagy promotes cellular renewal, removes damaged proteins, and supports healthy aging.

### Key Ingredients & Research Direction

**Potential Autophagy-Activating Ingredients:**
1. **Resveratrol** - Activates SIRT1 pathway, induces autophagy
2. **Spermidine** - Natural polyamine that induces autophagy
3. **Trehalose** - Disaccharide that activates autophagy
4. **EGCG (Green Tea)** - Polyphenol with autophagy-inducing properties
5. **Curcumin** - Activates autophagy pathways
6. **Rapamycin derivatives** - mTOR inhibitors (research stage)

**Research Needed:**
