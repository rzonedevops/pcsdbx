## STRATEGIC CONTEXT

**Why Niacinamide Matters:**
Niacinamide (Vitamin B3) is one of the most versatile and well-researched skincare actives, addressing multiple skin concerns including:
- **Hyperpigmentation:** Reduces melanin transfer, evens skin tone
- **Acne & Sebum Regulation:** Controls oil production, reduces breakouts
- **Aging & Fine Lines:** Stimulates collagen production, reduces wrinkles
- **Barrier Function:** Increases ceramide synthesis, strengthens skin barrier
- **Inflammation & Redness:** Anti-inflammatory properties, calms irritation

**Market Position:**
- One of the most popular skincare ingredients globally
- Extensive clinical research supporting multiple benefits (2-5% concentration)
- Compatible with most formulation types and other actives
- Essential for comprehensive formulation database
- Available in multiple purity grades (95%, 99%, 99.5%+)
