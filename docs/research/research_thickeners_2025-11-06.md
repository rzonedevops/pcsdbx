## Research Summary

Thickeners are essential formulation ingredients used to modify the viscosity and texture of personal care products. This category includes natural thickeners, rheology modifiers, gelling agents, and viscosity enhancers. The research identified 31 suppliers offering thickening agents for cosmetic and personal care formulations.

**Key Findings:**
- **31 total suppliers** identified in the Thickeners category
- Mix of major chemical distributors and specialized ingredient suppliers
- Strong representation of natural and sustainable thickening options
- Geographic diversity: USA, Canada, Europe, Asia, Latin America
- Product range: xanthan gum, guar gum, cellulose derivatives, waxes, silicones
