## Overview
Squalane is a highly stable, plant-based emollient that mimics the skin's natural lipids. It is derived from olive oil or sugarcane and is prized in clean beauty for its lightweight, non-greasy hydration properties. Squalane is biomimetic, non-comedogenic, and suitable for all skin types.
