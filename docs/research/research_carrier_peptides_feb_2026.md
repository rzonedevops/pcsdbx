## Research Notes

**Carrier Peptides Overview:**
- Carrier peptides deliver trace elements (copper, manganese, zinc) essential for enzymatic reactions
- GHK-Cu (Copper Tripeptide-1) is the most well-known and researched carrier peptide
- Benefits include wound healing, collagen synthesis, antioxidant defense, and anti-aging
- Liposomal delivery systems enhance penetration and efficacy

**Market Trends:**
- Growing demand for copper peptides in anti-aging formulations
- Increased focus on bioavailability and delivery systems (liposomes, nanoemulsions)
- Sustainability and eco-friendly production becoming important differentiators
- Quality control and purity (99%+) are critical for cosmetic applications
