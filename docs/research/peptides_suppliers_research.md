## PEPTIDE CATEGORIES & MECHANISMS

### 1. Signal Peptides (Collagen/Elastin Stimulation)

**Mechanism:** Stimulate fibroblast activity to increase collagen, elastin, and fibronectin synthesis

**Key Examples:**
- **Matrixyl (Palmitoyl Pentapeptide-4)** - Stimulates collagen types I, III, and IV
- **Matrixyl 3000** - Combination of palmitoyl oligopeptides for enhanced efficacy
- **Syn-Coll (Palmitoyl Tripeptide-5)** - Collagen synthesis booster
- **GHK-Cu (Copper Tripeptide-1)** - Wound healing and collagen synthesis

**Suppliers:** Sederma (Croda), Lipotec (Lubrizol), Caregen, Lucas Meyer Cosmetics

### 2. Carrier Peptides (Metal Ion Delivery)

**Mechanism:** Deliver essential trace elements (copper, manganese, zinc) to skin cells for enzymatic activity

**Key Examples:**
- **GHK-Cu (Copper Tripeptide-1)** - Copper delivery for wound healing and collagen synthesis
- **Manganese Tripeptide-1** - Manganese delivery for antioxidant enzyme activation
- **Zinc Peptides** - Zinc delivery for wound healing and anti-inflammatory effects

**Suppliers:** Lipotec, Caregen, Spec-Chem, Active Concepts

### 3. Neurotransmitter-Inhibiting Peptides (Muscle Relaxation)

**Mechanism:** Inhibit neurotransmitter release at neuromuscular junction, reducing muscle contraction and expression wrinkles

**Key Examples:**
- **Argireline (Acetyl Hexapeptide-8)** - SNARE complex inhibitor, "topical Botox"
- **Syn-Ake (Dipeptide Diaminobutyroyl Benzylamide Diacetate)** - Snake venom-inspired peptide
- **SNAP-8 (Acetyl Octapeptide-3)** - Enhanced version of Argireline
- **Acetyl Hexapeptide-3** - Alternative neurotransmitter inhibitor

**Suppliers:** Lipotec (Lubrizol), DSM, Mibelle Biochemistry, Silab

### 4. Specialized Peptides

**Categories:**
- **Antimicrobial Peptides** - Skin microbiome modulation and acne treatment
- **Barrier Repair Peptides** - Ceramide synthesis and barrier function enhancement
- **Pigmentation Peptides** - Melanin synthesis regulation and skin brightening
- **Anti-Inflammatory Peptides** - Cytokine modulation and inflammation reduction

**Suppliers:** Lipotec, Caregen, Provital, Symrise
