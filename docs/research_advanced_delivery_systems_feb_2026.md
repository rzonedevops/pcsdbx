## Executive Summary

Advanced Delivery Systems represent a critical frontier in cosmetic science, enabling formulators to dramatically enhance the efficacy, stability, and bioavailability of active ingredients. This category encompasses **liposomes**, **nanoemulsions**, **microspheres**, **solid lipid nanoparticles (SLNs)**, **nanostructured lipid carriers (NLCs)**, and other novel encapsulation technologies.

This is a **zero-listing category** in pcsdbx and must be established as a priority. The global cosmetic encapsulation market is valued at over $1.2 billion and growing at ~8% CAGR, making this one of the most commercially significant gaps in our database.
