## Risk Assessment

**Current Risks:**

1. **Slow Progress:** At current pace (4 listings in initial setup), reaching 313+ listings will take significant time
2. **Data Staleness:** Without automation, data will quickly become outdated
3. **Scope Creep:** Vision includes complex optimization and integration that may distract from core data collection
4. **Legal Compliance:** Need to verify compliance with website terms of service for scraping
5. **Resource Constraints:** Manual data entry is not scalable

**Mitigation Strategies:**

1. Implement automation immediately to accelerate data collection
2. Focus on data volume before depth in early phases
3. Establish clear phase gates to prevent premature optimization
4. Review and comply with website terms of service
5. Leverage AI agents for scalable data processing
