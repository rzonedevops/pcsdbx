# Bakuchiol Supplier Research - November 19, 2025

## Overview
Bakuchiol is a natural, plant-based retinol alternative derived from Psoralea corylifolia (Babchi plant). It has gained significant popularity in the cosmetics industry as a gentler alternative to retinol with similar anti-aging benefits but without photosensitivity or irritation.

## Quality Considerations

### Purity Matters
- **Pure Bakuchiol (99%+):** Clinically proven, safe, effective
- **Psoralea Corylifolia Extract:** Contains furocoumarins (photosensitizers), NOT suitable for cosmetics
- **Verification:** HPLC testing recommended to verify actual Bakuchiol content

### Safety
- Pure Bakuchiol (99%+): Safe for cosmetic use
- Plant extracts: May contain photosensitizers, highly regulated in Europe
- Always verify purity and source
