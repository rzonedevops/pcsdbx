# pH Adjusters Supplier Research - December 12, 2025

## Research Objective
Identify 10-15 high-quality suppliers of cosmetic pH adjusters (sodium hydroxide, citric acid, lactic acid, TEA, sodium bicarbonate, potassium hydroxide) for the pcsdbx personal care supplier database.

## Strategic Value
pH adjusters are critical for formulation pH optimization, ensuring product stability, efficacy, and skin compatibility. Essential for every cosmetic formulation to maintain optimal pH range (typically 4.5-6.5 for skincare).

## Key pH Adjuster Types
- **Alkaline Adjusters:** Sodium Hydroxide (caustic soda), Potassium Hydroxide, TEA (Triethanolamine), Sodium Bicarbonate
- **Acidic Adjusters:** Citric Acid, Lactic Acid, Phosphoric Acid, Tartaric Acid
- **Buffer Systems:** Citrate buffers, phosphate buffers, acetate buffers
