## 1. SPRAY BOTTLES

### Definition
Spray bottles are containers with pump or mist spray mechanisms used for dispensing liquids in fine mist or controlled spray patterns. Commonly used for toners, setting sprays, body sprays, and other liquid personal care products.

### Types of Spray Bottles
- **Fine Mist Sprayers** - Produces ultra-fine mist for facial toners and setting sprays
- **Pump Sprayers** - Controlled spray for body sprays and perfumes
- **Continuous Sprayers** - Consistent spray pattern for hair products
- **Airless Sprayers** - Preserves product integrity and prevents contamination

## 2. ROLL-ON CONTAINERS

### Definition
Roll-on containers feature a rolling ball applicator mechanism for precise, hands-free application. Commonly used for deodorants, serums, essential oils, and therapeutic products.

### Types of Roll-On Containers
- **Standard Roll-On Bottles** - Basic roller ball design for deodorants and perfumes
- **Airless Roller Ball Bottles** - Prevents contamination and extends shelf life
- **Precision Roller Ball Applicators** - Multiple roller balls for massage effect
- **Glass Roll-On Bottles** - Premium option for essential oils and serums

## 3. COMPACTS

### Definition
Compacts are small, portable containers for powdered or solid cosmetic products such as pressed powders, blushes, eyeshadows, and bronzers. They typically feature a hinged lid with an integrated mirror.

### Types of Compacts
- **Powder Compacts** - For pressed powders and setting powders
- **Blush Compacts** - Designed for blush and bronzer products
- **Eyeshadow Palettes** - Multiple compartments for eyeshadow colors
- **Compact Cases with Mirrors** - Premium options with integrated mirrors
- **Airtight Compacts** - Sealed designs for product preservation
