## Research Overview

**Why This Category Matters:**
Ceramides are premium barrier repair actives essential for skin health. These lipid molecules are critical components of the skin barrier, with proven efficacy in treating dry skin, eczema, and barrier dysfunction. Ceramides represent a high-value ingredient category with strong clinical evidence and growing market demand for barrier repair and skin health formulations.

**Key Ingredient Types:**
- Ceramide NP (most common type)
- Ceramide AP (alpha-hydroxy fatty acid)
- Ceramide EOP (ester-linked omega-hydroxy fatty acid)
- Phytoceramides (plant-derived alternatives)
- Synthetic ceramides
- Ceramide complexes (multi-ceramide blends)

**Clinical Benefits:**
- Skin barrier repair and strengthening
- Moisture retention and hydration
- Reduction of transepidermal water loss (TEWL)
- Treatment of dry skin and eczema
- Anti-aging and skin smoothing
- Protection against environmental stressors
