## Research Overview

**Why This Category Matters:**
Probiotics and fermented ingredients represent the microbiome revolution in skincare. These ingredients are among the fastest-growing categories in personal care, with proven benefits for skin barrier function, inflammation reduction, and microbiome balance. Essential for SkinTwin's advanced formulation capabilities in next-generation skincare.

**Key Ingredient Types:**
- Probiotic lysates (bifida ferment lysate, lactobacillus ferment)
- Fermented extracts (galactomyces ferment filtrate, sake kasu)
- Postbiotics (metabolites, cell fragments)
- Prebiotics (microbiome food sources)
- Microbiome-friendly ingredients
