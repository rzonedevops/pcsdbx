## CATEGORY 1: BLUE LIGHT PROTECTION ACTIVES

### Overview

Blue light (400-500nm wavelength) penetrates deeper than UVB, triggering oxidative stress, accelerating aging, causing pigmentation, and compromising barrier function. Protection mechanisms include:

- **Light Absorption/Filtering** - Carotenoids, melanin, iron oxides
- **Antioxidant Defense** - Neutralizing blue light-induced free radicals
- **DNA Protection** - Preventing cellular damage and photoaging
- **Barrier Strengthening** - Supporting skin's natural defense systems

- **Antioxidant Defense** - Neutralizing pollution-induced free radicals
- **Anti-inflammatory** - Reducing pollution-triggered inflammation
- **Barrier Repair** - Strengthening skin's natural protective function
