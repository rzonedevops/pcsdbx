## Market Context

Adaptogens are a rapidly growing category in skincare formulations, driven by consumer interest in holistic, wellness-focused beauty products. These ingredients help skin adapt to stress (environmental, oxidative, inflammatory) and support skin barrier function, anti-aging, and overall skin health.

**Key Adaptogen Types:**
- **Botanical Adaptogens:** Rhodiola, Ashwagandha, Ginseng, Holy Basil, Schisandra
- **Mushroom Adaptogens:** Reishi, Cordyceps, Lion's Mane, Chaga
- **Other:** Maca, Astragalus
