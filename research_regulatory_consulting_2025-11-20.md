# Regulatory Consulting Services Research - November 20, 2025

## Overview
Regulatory consulting is CRITICAL for cosmetic manufacturers to navigate complex FDA (USA) and EU regulations, ensure compliance, avoid product recalls, and successfully launch products in global markets. Professional regulatory consultants provide expertise in:
- Product registration and notification
- Safety assessments and documentation
- Claims substantiation and validation
- Labeling compliance
- Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) implementation
- International market entry support

## Market Context
The cosmetic regulatory landscape is increasingly complex:
- FDA regulations (USA): 21 CFR Parts 700-740
- EU Cosmetics Regulation (EC) No 1223/2009
- China NMPA registration requirements
- ASEAN Cosmetic Directive
- Growing demand for clean beauty and transparency
- Increased enforcement and penalties for non-compliance
