## Business Description

Oat Cosmetics researches, develops, and supplies natural ingredients derived mainly from oats for the beauty and personal care market. These ingredients are sold globally through a network of specialized distributors with expertise in the cosmetic sector. All ingredients are produced to the highest standards using modern, sustainable farming practices to ensure full traceability from field to face.
