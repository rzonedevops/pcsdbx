## 1. PRESSURE-SENSITIVE LABELS (PSL)

## 3. WRAP-AROUND LABELS

### Definition
Wrap-around labels are traditional labels that wrap around the circumference of a container. They are commonly used on cylindrical bottles and jars.

### Key Suppliers Identified

**Note:** Wrap-around labels are often offered as part of pressure-sensitive label services. Key suppliers include:

- **CCL Label** - Offers wrap-around solutions as part of their PSL portfolio
- **Premium Label & Packaging** - Custom wrap-around label manufacturing
- **Taylor** - Wrap-around label solutions
- **Avery Dennison** - Global wrap-around label options
- **Wausau Coated** - Wrap-around label materials and coatings
- **Meyers** - Wrap-around label design and printing

### Research Notes
- Wrap-around labels are typically pressure-sensitive or adhesive-based
- Commonly used for cylindrical bottles, jars, and containers
- Can be customized for specific container dimensions
- Often combined with other label types in supplier portfolios

## 4. RFID LABELS

### Definition
RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) labels incorporate microchip technology for product tracking, authentication, and supply chain management. These are increasingly used in premium personal care products.

### Key Suppliers Identified

**Note:** RFID labels are a specialized segment. Initial research indicates:

- **Avery Dennison** - Offers RFID solutions for various industries
- **Zebra Technologies** - RFID label manufacturing and solutions
- **Alien Technology** - RFID inlay and label manufacturing
- **Impinj** - RFID solutions and technology

### Research Notes
- RFID labels are less common in mainstream personal care but growing in premium segments
- Used for anti-counterfeiting and supply chain tracking
- Higher cost than traditional labels
- Requires compatible RFID reading infrastructure
