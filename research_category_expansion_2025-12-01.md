## 1. Raw Materials: Adaptogens

### Category Description
Adaptogenic ingredients are plant-based compounds that help the skin adapt to stressors and maintain balance. Key adaptogens in skincare include ashwagandha, rhodiola, ginseng, and other botanical extracts.

### Key Suppliers Identified

**Ingredient Suppliers:**
- **Active Concepts LLC** (activeconceptsllc.com) - Specializes in innovative and sustainable personal care ingredients, including adaptogenic compounds
- **Symrise** (symrise.com) - Offers SymReboot™ L19 and other probiotic-based adaptogenic ingredients for cosmetics
- **Glenn Corp** (glenncorp.com) - Provides adaptogen products for personal care and cosmetic applications
- **Sunrise Global Trading** (sunriseglobaltrading.com) - High-quality adaptogenic herbs and herbal extracts including ashwagandha, rhodiola, and ginseng
- **Gaia Herbs** - Focuses on herbal extracts with transparent sourcing
- **Evolva** - Specializes in bio-based ingredients, including adaptogenic compounds
- **Adaptology** (adaptology.com) - Natural & organic adaptogenic skincare products and ingredients
- **LBB Specialties** (lbbspecialties.com) - Care ingredient and raw material suppliers with adaptogenic formulations

### Product Mapping Intelligence
- Ashwagandha (KSM-66 standardized extract) is widely used in premium skincare formulations
- Rhodiola and ginseng complexes are commonly found in anti-aging and stress-relief skincare
- Adaptogenic serums are trending in the natural and organic skincare segment

### Pricing Intelligence Notes
- Premium adaptogenic ingredients command higher price points
- Standardized extracts (like KSM-66 ashwagandha) are more expensive than raw plant materials
- Bulk pricing available for manufacturers; request-a-quote model is common

## 2. Raw Materials: Prebiotics

### Category Description
Prebiotics are compounds that feed and nurture beneficial bacteria on the skin, supporting the skin microbiota and strengthening the skin barrier. Common prebiotics include inulin, FOS (fructooligosaccharides), and alpha-glucan oligosaccharides.

### Key Suppliers Identified

**Ingredient Suppliers:**
- **Ultra Chemical Inc.** (ulprospector.com) - BIOLIN/P prebiotic based on chicory root inulin
- **DKSH** (ulprospector.com) - preBIULIN FOS prebiotic sugar for skin microbiota control
- **Symrise** (symrise.com) - Probiotic-derived personal care ingredients with prebiotic components
- **HeiQ Synbio** (heiq.com) - Synbiotics combining probiotics and prebiotics for personal care
- **IndoGulf BioAg** (indogulfbioag.com) - Biotech-derived ingredients including fermented hyaluronic acid and probiotic lysates
- **LBB Specialties** (lbbspecialties.com) - Bonicel, a probiotic-derived personal care ingredient
- **Aleavia** (aleavia.com) - Organic skincare with prebiotic ingredients
- **Formula Botanica** (formulabotanica.com) - Fermented prebiotic components for skincare formulations

### Product Mapping Intelligence
- Prebiotic skincare is emerging as a major trend in microbiome-focused beauty products
- Chicory root-derived inulin is the most common prebiotic ingredient in cosmetics
- Synbiotics (probiotics + prebiotics) are gaining traction in premium skincare lines

### Pricing Intelligence Notes
- Prebiotic ingredients are moderately priced compared to other active ingredients
- Fermented prebiotic components command premium pricing
- Volume discounts available for cosmetic manufacturers
