2. **Leave Clear Messages**
   - Use descriptive filenames with dates: `message_YYYY-MM-DD_[agent]_[topic].md`

   - Celebrate wins and acknowledge good work!

4. **Document Progress**
   - Share milestone achievements
   - Report completion of major research phases
   - Highlight interesting supplier discoveries
   - Note any challenges or blockers
