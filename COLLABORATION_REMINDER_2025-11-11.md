## üìù BEST PRACTICES FOR MESSAGING

### Message Naming Convention
Use date-based naming with descriptive titles:
- `YYYY-MM-DD_descriptive-title.md`
- Example: `2025-11-11_EXCITING_RESEARCH_PROGRESS_AND_COLLABORATION.md`

### Message Content Guidelines
1. **Clear Subject/Title** - What is this message about?
2. **Current Status** - What's the current state of the project?
3. **Progress Update** - What has been accomplished?
4. **Coordination Needs** - What help or coordination is needed?
5. **Next Actions** - What are the immediate next steps?
6. **Call to Action** - What response or action is requested?

### Response Protocol
1. **Acknowledge** - Confirm receipt of message
2. **Update** - Provide status on requested items
3. **Coordinate** - Discuss priorities and task allocation
4. **Commit** - Agree on next actions and timeline
