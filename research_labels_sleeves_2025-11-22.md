## Category 1: Pressure-Sensitive Labels (PSL)

### Overview
Pressure-sensitive labels are the most common label type in personal care packaging. They use adhesive backing that bonds to surfaces when pressure is applied, without requiring heat, water, or solvents. PSL offers versatility in materials (paper, film, metalized), finishes, and embellishments.

## Category 2: Shrink Sleeves

### Overview
Shrink sleeve labels are made from heat-shrinkable film (typically PVC, PETG, or OPS) that conforms to the shape of containers when heat is applied. They provide 360° decoration, making them ideal for shaped bottles and maximum visual impact. Shrink sleeves are increasingly popular in personal care packaging for their design flexibility and tamper-evidence capabilities.
