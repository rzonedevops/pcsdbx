## Research Notes

### Market Segments
1. **High-Speed Industrial Lines:** Groninger (240 units/min), Optima, Landpack
2. **Mid-Range Flexible Systems:** Volumetric Technologies, E-PAK, Accutek
3. **Integrated Monobloc Solutions:** CDA USA (E-Fill 3-in-1)
4. **Small-Scale/Startup Equipment:** Zonesun, Makwell manual systems
5. **Turnkey Solutions:** Optima, PMI Kyoto, BellatRx

### Key Technologies
- **Piston Filling:** High precision for viscous products (Volumetric Technologies)
- **Volumetric Filling:** Consistent volume control (Makwell)
- **Pump Filling:** Servo-driven for accuracy (Volumetric Technologies, E-PAK)
- **Overflow Filling:** For liquid products (Volumetric Technologies)
- **Monobloc Systems:** Integrated fill-cap-label (CDA USA)

### Geographic Distribution
- **USA:** Volumetric Technologies (Minnesota), E-PAK, Accutek, Weighpack
- **Europe:** Groninger (Germany), Optima (Germany), CDA (France)
- **Asia:** Landpack (China), Zonesun (China)
- **Global:** PMI Kyoto, BellatRx

### Critical Features for Cosmetics
1. **Hygiene:** Stainless steel construction, easy cleaning
2. **Viscosity Handling:** Creams, lotions, serums, oils
3. **Precision:** Consistent fill volumes, minimal waste
4. **Flexibility:** Quick changeovers, multiple container types
5. **Scalability:** From startup to high-volume production
6. **Integration:** Complete lines (filling + capping + labeling)
