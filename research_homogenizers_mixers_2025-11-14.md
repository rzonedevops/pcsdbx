## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Homogenizers and mixers are essential equipment for personal care and cosmetics manufacturing, critical for creating stable emulsions, dispersing ingredients, and ensuring product uniformity. This research documents suppliers offering high-shear mixers, homogenizers, laboratory mixers, production mixers, rotor-stator mixers, and inline homogenizers.

**Key Findings:**
- 20+ suppliers identified across laboratory and production-scale equipment
- Major international manufacturers with North American presence
- Specializations range from bench-top laboratory mixers to automated turnkey solutions
- Equipment types include: high-shear mixers, rotor-stator homogenizers, vacuum homogenizers, batch mixers, inline mixers
- Capacity ranges from 250ml (laboratory) to 20 tons (production)
