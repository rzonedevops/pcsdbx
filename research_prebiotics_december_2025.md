## Market Context

Prebiotics are a rapidly emerging category in skincare, driven by the growing understanding of the skin microbiome's role in skin health. Prebiotics serve as "food" for beneficial skin bacteria, supporting microbiome balance, skin barrier function, and overall skin health. This category is synergistic with probiotics and postbiotics, forming the complete microbiome skincare ecosystem.

**Key Prebiotic Types:**
- **Oligosaccharides:** Inulin, FOS (Fructooligosaccharides), GOS (Galactooligosaccharides), XOS (Xylooligosaccharides)
- **Alpha-Glucan Oligosaccharide:** Microbiome-balancing prebiotic
- **Sugars:** Rhamnose, Lactose, Lactulose
- **Polysaccharides:** Maltodextrin, Resistant Starch
