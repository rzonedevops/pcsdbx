## OVERVIEW

Vitamin C is one of the most popular and extensively researched active ingredients in skincare. Multiple forms exist with different stability profiles, pH requirements, and efficacy characteristics. This research documents suppliers of vitamin C and its derivatives for cosmetic formulation.

### Vitamin C Forms Researched
1. **L-Ascorbic Acid** - Pure vitamin C, most potent but least stable
2. **Magnesium Ascorbyl Phosphate (MAP)** - Stable, water-soluble derivative
3. **Sodium Ascorbyl Phosphate (SAP)** - Stable, water-soluble derivative
4. **Ascorbyl Glucoside (AA2G)** - Stable, water-soluble derivative
5. **Tetrahexyldecyl Ascorbate (THDA)** - Stable, oil-soluble derivative
6. **Ethyl Ascorbic Acid (Et-VC)** - Stable, water-soluble derivative

## VITAMIN C FORMS SUMMARY

### L-Ascorbic Acid (Pure Vitamin C)
- **Suppliers:** DSM-Firmenich, Making Cosmetics, Vivion Inc.
- **Characteristics:** Most potent, least stable, pH 2-3.5 required
- **Applications:** High-potency serums, professional treatments

### Magnesium Ascorbyl Phosphate (MAP)
- **Suppliers:** Making Cosmetics, COSROMA, Princeton Powder, Plamed
- **Characteristics:** Stable, water-soluble, pH 6-7, light-stable, oxygen-stable
- **Applications:** Whitening, anti-wrinkle, anti-aging products

### Sodium Ascorbyl Phosphate (SAP)
- **Suppliers:** BASF Personal Care, Making Cosmetics
- **Characteristics:** Stable, water-soluble, pH 6-7, light-stable
- **Applications:** Brightening serums, anti-acne formulations

### Ascorbyl Glucoside (AA2G)
- **Suppliers:** Macsen Laboratories, Bramble Berry, Derma Factors, CocoJojo
- **Characteristics:** Stable, water-soluble, gentle, converts to ascorbic acid in skin
- **Applications:** Sensitive skin formulations, brightening products

### Tetrahexyldecyl Ascorbate (THDA)
- **Suppliers:** Lotion Crafter
- **Characteristics:** Stable, oil-soluble, non-irritating
- **Applications:** Oil-based serums, anhydrous formulations

### Ethyl Ascorbic Acid (Et-VC)
- **Suppliers:** Corum Inc.
- **Characteristics:** Stable, water-soluble, excellent whitening and collagen boosting
- **Applications:** Whitening products, anti-aging formulations
