## Key Scientific Insights

### Microbiome Revolution
The cosmetic industry is experiencing a paradigm shift from "kill all bacteria" (traditional preservatives) to "nurture beneficial bacteria" (microbiome-friendly approach).

### Three Categories of Microbiome Actives:
1. **Prebiotics:** Feed beneficial bacteria (inulin, alpha-glucan oligosaccharide, FOS)
2. **Probiotics:** Live beneficial bacteria or their lysates (Lactobacillus, Bifida, Saccharomyces)
3. **Postbiotics:** Metabolites and secretions from beneficial bacteria (fermentation supernatants, metabolites)

### Key Strains in Cosmetics:
- **Lactobacillus:** L. rhamnosus, L. plantarum, L. acidophilus, L. crispatus
- **Bifida:** B. longum, B. bifidum
- **Saccharomyces:** S. cerevisiae

### Benefits Documented:
- Barrier repair and strengthening
- Inflammation reduction
- Skin hydration and elasticity
- Anti-aging effects
- Acne management
- Odor control (microbiome-friendly deodorants)
- Dandruff reduction

### Regulatory Considerations:
- EU Cosmetics Regulation compliance
- Microbiome-friendly preservation systems
- Safety testing for skin microbiota
- Claims substantiation
