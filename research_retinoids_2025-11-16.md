## Market Insights

### Retinoid Types Identified
1. **Retinol:** Pure vitamin A, most common form
2. **Bakuchiol:** Natural retinol alternative (Sabinsa)
3. **Encapsulated Retinol:** Nano-technology delivery (Bettech Nano Solutions)
4. **Time-Release Retinol:** Sustained delivery systems
5. **Retinyl Palmitate:** Gentler retinoid ester
6. **Retinaldehyde:** More potent than retinol
