## Research Summary

Emulsifiers are essential formulation ingredients that enable the mixing of oil and water phases in personal care products. This category includes natural emulsifiers, cold process emulsifiers, polymeric emulsifiers, and specialty emulsifying systems. The research identified 58 suppliers offering emulsifiers for cosmetic and personal care formulations.

**Key Findings:**
- **58 total suppliers** identified in the Emulsifiers category
- Mix of major ingredient manufacturers, distributors, and specialty suppliers
- Strong representation of natural and plant-based emulsifying systems
- Geographic diversity: USA, Europe, Asia, Latin America
- Product range: lecithin, wax esters, polymeric emulsifiers, cold process systems
