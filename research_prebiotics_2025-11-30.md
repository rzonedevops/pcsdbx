**Key Applications:**

- Natural and clean beauty products
- Pollution protection formulations

**Supplier Landscape:**
- Biotech innovators (HeiQ, IndoGulf) focus on patented technologies and fermentation
- Specialized microbiome brands (Nuebiome, Gallinée) focus on complete microbiome solutions
- Natural ingredient suppliers (Aromantic, Aleavia) focus on organic and clean sourcing
- Distribution platforms (Ulprospector, LBB Specialties) provide access to diverse prebiotic options

### Key Prebiotic Ingredients

1. **Inulin (from Chicory Root)** - Most common prebiotic for microbiome support
2. **FOS (Fructooligosaccharides)** - Prebiotic fiber for microbiota nourishment
3. **Glucan** - Beta-glucan for barrier support and microbiome balance
4. **Oat Extract** - Gentle prebiotic for sensitive skin
5. **Synbiotics** - Combination of prebiotics and probiotics for enhanced effect
6. **Bioferments** - Fermented prebiotic ingredients with enhanced bioavailability
7. **Plant-Based Prebiotics** - Various plant extracts with prebiotic properties
