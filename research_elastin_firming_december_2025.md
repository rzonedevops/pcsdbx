## Market Context

### Why Elastin & Firming Actives Matter

**Skin Aging Science:**
- Elastin provides skin elasticity and bounce-back properties
- Elastin degradation leads to sagging, loss of firmness, wrinkles
- Elastin production declines significantly with age (more than collagen)
- Firming actives address visible signs of aging: sagging, loss of contour, wrinkles

**Market Trends:**
- Growing demand for comprehensive anti-aging solutions (collagen + elastin)
- Shift toward multi-mechanism firming approaches
- Biomimetic and biotechnology-derived elastin gaining traction
- Clinical validation increasingly important for premium positioning

**Formulation Applications:**
- Anti-aging serums and creams
