## Research Summary

Humectants are essential moisturizing ingredients that attract and retain water in personal care formulations. This category includes glycerin, hyaluronic acid, propylene glycol, sorbitol, and other water-binding ingredients. The research identified 13 suppliers offering humectant ingredients for cosmetic and personal care formulations.

**Key Findings:**
- **13 total suppliers** identified in the Humectants category
- Mix of major chemical distributors and specialized ingredient suppliers
- Strong representation of natural and sustainable humectant options
- Geographic diversity: USA, Europe, Asia, Australia
- Product range: glycerin, propylene glycol, sorbitol, hyaluronic acid, sodium PCA, plant-based humectants
