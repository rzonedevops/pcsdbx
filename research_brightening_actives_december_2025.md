## CATEGORY 1: ALPHA ARBUTIN SUPPLIERS

### Overview
Alpha Arbutin (4-Hydroxyphenyl-Œ±-D-glucopyranoside) is a biosynthetic, water-soluble skin brightening active that inhibits tyrosinase and melanin synthesis. It's considered safer and more stable than hydroquinone, with fewer side effects.

**CAS Number:** 84380-01-8  
**INCI Name:** Alpha-Arbutin  
**Typical Usage:** 0.5-2% (efficacy), up to 5% (premium formulations)  
**Applications:** Serums, creams, toners, brightening treatments, dark spot correctors
