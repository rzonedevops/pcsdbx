## Research Summary

UV filters and sunscreen actives represent a critical category for sun care formulations. This category includes both mineral (physical) filters like zinc oxide and titanium dioxide, as well as chemical (organic) UV filters. The market is increasingly focused on reef-safe, broad-spectrum protection with natural and sustainable options.

**Key Market Trends:**
- Mineral sunscreens (zinc oxide, titanium dioxide) gaining popularity
- Reef-safe formulations increasingly required
- Broad-spectrum UVA/UVB protection standard
- Natural and biodegradable UV filters emerging
- Regulatory complexity (FDA, EU regulations)
- Hybrid formulations (mineral + chemical) growing
